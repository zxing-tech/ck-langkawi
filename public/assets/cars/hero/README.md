# Hero Section Car Images

Add 3 car images here for the hero slider:

- `car-1.png` - Recommended: Luxury sedan or sports car
- `car-2.png` - Recommended: SUV or family car
- `car-3.png` - Recommended: Premium or electric vehicle

## Image Specifications:
- **Dimensions**: 364 x 512 pixels (portrait orientation)
- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **Background**: Clean/transparent background preferred
- **Quality**: High resolution for best display

## Tips:
- Use professional car photos with good lighting
- Show the car from an attractive angle (3/4 front view works best)
- Ensure consistent styling across all 3 images
- Use placeholder images from https://placehold.co/364x512 if needed temporarily
